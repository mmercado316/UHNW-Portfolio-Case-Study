## What the Prospect File actually solves

The Prospect File reframes top-of-funnel work as fit-diagnosis first, capability demonstration second.

Wealthy investors weigh five criteria when evaluating advisors — People, Philosophy, Process, Performance, Fees — and the rank-ordering varies meaningfully across prospects (3). Outreach calibrated to the heaviest weighted P converts; outreach calibrated to the wrong P does not. The Prospect File's discipline is to identify that P before the first touch and shape every subsequent move around it.

Durable fit between advisor and client (*relationship alpha*) is more predictive of long-term retention than investment performance (4). Prospecting at UHNW scale is a fit-diagnosis exercise first and a capability demonstration second.

67% of affluent prospects who hired an advisor already knew someone with that advisor before the relationship began (5). The Prospect File treats the relational graph (centers of influence, alumni networks, fellow board members, trusted COIs) as a first-class citizen.

The three layers (*Profile* for what the prospect is, *Decision Log* for what the banker has thought and done, *Relational Graph* for the connective tissue of COIs and pattern matches) together convert a prospect's signal set into a single-sentence outreach thesis: the right opening to the right person at the right moment.

Prospect Files together form a *pipeline*: qualification at the top, multi-meeting depth in the middle, existing-client relationships at the far end. The same Prospect File travels with each relationship across stages; the discipline that won the relationship is the discipline that keeps it.

## What the Prospect File takes in

Inputs are two kinds of signal. The first is conversational: what the prospect volunteered at a dinner, a panel, a child's school event; what they avoided; what they returned to twice; who they referenced by first name versus last name. The second is public: filings, board seats, recent liquidity events, philanthropic activity, civic involvement, published interviews, and the connective tissue between these.

Neither signal type is useful alone. A standalone public-data pull produces a dossier. A standalone conversational note produces a memory. The cross-reference produces an outreach decision.

## The workflow in practice

Every first encounter logs to the Prospect File within 24 hours. Conversational signals go in verbatim. Public signals refresh monthly via a research-compression pass. Every prospect carries a single-sentence outreach thesis answering one question: what is the right opening for this specific prospect? Outreach moves only when the thesis is clean.
