# Closing bridge (revised)

*Removes the v1 closing's three-question structure entirely. Reasoning: (1) the four Prospect Files + strategic memo + extensibility sidebar already make every argument a closing thesis would try to make, and adding a thesis statement after a complete case study signals the writer isn't sure the work stands on its own; (2) for Mario's audience (senior UHNW bankers evaluating him as a candidate), a declarative craft thesis would require a voice-of-craft authority he is trying to earn, not claim; (3) open-question closers read as essay-literary rather than banker-craft. Replaces the closing with a single bridge line into Notes & Sources. At step 8 deliverable assembly, this line renders as a transitional sentence before the Notes & Sources section, not a separate section with its own header.*
