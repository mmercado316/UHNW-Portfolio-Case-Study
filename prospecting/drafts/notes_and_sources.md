Final count: 9 sources.*

---

1. Altrata, *The Wealthy in San Francisco 2023*. https://altrata.com/reports/wealthy-in-san-francisco-2023. SF metro UHNW population of ~4,380; 84 billionaires; seventh-highest global UHNW density per capita.
2. The Spokesman-Review, summarizing Joint Venture Silicon Valley and Kauffman Foundation data (March 2025). https://www.spokesman.com/stories/2025/mar/11/two-thirds-of-silicon-valley-tech-workers-are-fore/. ~66% foreign-born tech workforce; ~45% of Bay Area tech startups have at least one foreign-born founder.
3. Charlotte Beyer, *Wealth Management Unwrapped* (Wiley, rev. 2017). https://www.amazon.com/Wealth-Management-Unwrapped-Revised-Expanded/dp/1119403693. The Five Ps framework (People, Philosophy, Process, Performance, Fees) and the practitioner instruction to have prospects rank-order them as a diagnostic.
4. Charlotte Beyer, "Relationship Alpha: The Emerging Competitive Advantage in Wealth Management," CFA Institute Research Foundation Brief, 2019. https://rpc.cfainstitute.org/research/foundation/2019/beyer-brief. The argument that durable advisor-client fit is more predictive of long-term retention than investment alpha.
5. Matt Oechsli, "The #1 Reason Affluent Prospects Select Their Advisor" and related Oechsli Institute research. https://oechsli.com/. 67% of affluent hires already know someone with the advisor, which establishes relational warmth as the dominant UHNW conversion pathway.
6. Jaffe & Grubman, *Cross Cultures: How Global Families Negotiate Change Across Generations* (2016). https://dennisjaffe.com/cross-cultures-how-global-families-negotiate-change-across-generations/. Three-culture model (Individualist, Collective Harmony, Honor) used in prospect decision-mode classification.
7. Tim Kochis, *Managing Concentrated Stock Wealth: An Advisor's Guide to Building Customized Solutions* (Bloomberg, 2015). https://www.amazon.com/Managing-Concentrated-Stock-Wealth-Customized/dp/1119131588. The canonical argument that most tax-alpha opportunities (10b5-1 sequencing, QSBS stacking, exchange funds) exist pre-liquidity-event; the 6-18-month pre-event planning window referenced in Prospect 1's outreach play.
8. Thayer Willis, *Navigating the Dark Side of Wealth*. https://www.thayerwillis.com/. Wealth-psychology framework identifying inheritor identity patterns; the diagnostic significance of third-person wealth language used in Prospect 3's analytical core.
9. Michael Kitces, "The 5-Step Not-Cheesy Prospecting Process." https://www.kitces.com/blog/prospect-process-advisor-value-client-relationship-trust-building/. Canonical advisor sales pipeline (Initial Inquiry → Learn More Call → Discovery Meeting → 1-Page Plan → Plan Presentation), source for the funnel-stage naming used in the opening (targeting, outreach, discovery, advancement).
